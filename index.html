MY RECIPE

KHEEER

GO THROUGH FOLLOWING STEPS...


INGREDIENTS NEEDED

  • milk
    • 1cup
  • water
    • 1/2cup
  • sugar
    • 1/2cup(add as per taste)
  • dry fruits
    1. khaju
    2. bhaddam
    3. kissmiss
  • sammya(roasted)

PROCEEDURE

  1. Pour the water and milk into a vessel and boil them for 5 minutes
  2. Add sugar to it and keep boiling for 2 minutes
  3. Add sammya and cook it for 15minutes
  4. roast the dry fruits by adding ghee in another vessel
  5. severe the whole thing into a dish
  6. YUMMY!Kheer is ready to eat
